# 👁️ LumaVision — Smart Optical Store Management Platform

> *"Voyez votre magasin autrement"*

LumaVision is a centralized, intelligent management platform designed specifically for optical stores. Built by **Team AuraVerse** at ESPRIT (Honoris United Universities), it replaces fragmented, manual workflows with a modern, ergonomic solution that brings real-time visibility and smart automation to optical retail.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Optical stores currently suffer from fragmented, manual management leading to:

- **Frequent errors** from manual data entry
- **Time loss** due to scattered, time-consuming tasks
- **Poor inventory visibility** and inefficient planning

---

## 💡 Solution

LumaVision stands out from classic solutions through the integration of **real-time intelligent management** and **IoT modules** tailored to the optical sector.

### Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🏪 **Centralized Platform** | Complete management of stock, clients, sales, suppliers, and employees |
| 🖥️ **Ergonomic Interface** | Fast onboarding, reduced human error |
| 📊 **Smart Management** | Real-time tracking, alerts, and notifications to optimize workflow |
| 🔔 **Intelligent Alerts** | Anticipate problems and improve organization |
| ⚡ **Real-Time Dashboard** | Intuitive data board with instant insights |

---

## 🗂️ Modules

- **Employee Management** — Add, edit, delete, export employee records; stats by role
- **Client Management** — Full client database with loyalty tracking and product recommendations
- **Supplier Management** — Supplier directory with QR code generation, anomaly detection, and category stats
- **Stock Management** — Inventory tracking with safety stock alerts, category breakdown, and export
- **Sales Management** — Sales records with VAT, discounts, monthly trends, and detailed sale lines

| Store Size | Price (DT) | Includes |
|---|---|---|
